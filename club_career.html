Messi

Club career

Messi played a total of 778 official games for the Barcelona first team, scoring 672 goals and providing 305 assists. He won 10 Laliga Titles, 7 Copa del Reys, 4 UEFA Champion, 3 European Super Cups and 3 Club World Cups.

In terms of personal honors, Messi won 6 Ballon d'Or, 6 World Footballer of the Year, 2 UEFA Player of the Year, 6 European Golden Boots, 8 La Liga Golden Boots, and 10 European Championships during his time at Barcelona. Football League Team of the Year, 8 times selected FIFA Team of the Year.
